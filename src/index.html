Floattown

Welcome to Floattown.

About 10 years ago in the year 1802, land prices in the Kingdom of Podotia had gotten so high that the government approved cities to be built on water. One of the first floating cities to pop up was Floattown, founded and funded by entrepeneur Kadisc Letempyl.

Podotia is on the planet of Lomi, which has several other countries like Verkes, Shezhland, etc. In fact, Ome's (Marlulos), JJ's (Jose City), Smart's (New Perken), and Jaebom's (Nórawkaló) cities are also located on Lomi. But Ome made Marlulos in Roblox Studio, Floattown is entirely in lego. Although the official language of Podotia is Podotian, English is used in Floattown.

Both the lego city and this website were entirely created myself.
