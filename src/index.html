Flijto

Welcome to Flijto.

About 10 years ago in the year 1802, land prices in the Kingdom of Podotia had gotten so high that the government approved cities to be built on water. One of the first floating cities to pop up was Flijto, founded and funded by entrepeneur Kadisc Letempyl.

Podotia is on the planet of Lomi, which has a lot more countries like Verkes, Shezhland, etc. In fact, Ome's (Marlulos), JJ's (Jose City), Smart's (Perken), and Jaebom's (Nórawkaló) cities are also located on Lomi. But while Marlulos is in Roblox, Flijto is entirely in lego. The official language of Podotia is Podotian, but English is used in Flijto.
